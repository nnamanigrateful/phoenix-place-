About Phoenix

To provide a compassionate space for growth, healing, and self-discover

Welcome I'm a relational integrative psychotherapist, and I offer a warm, collaborative space where you can explore what’s troubling you in the context of a safe, supportive relationship. My approach is grounded in the belief that healing happens through connection—both with ourselves and with others. Drawing from a range of therapeutic traditions, I work in a way that’s tailored to you. Whether you're navigating anxiety, relationship challenges, trauma, or questions about identity and meaning, we can work together to better understand your patterns, deepen self-awareness, and support lasting change. .
